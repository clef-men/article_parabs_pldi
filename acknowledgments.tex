\begin{acks}

The implementation of \Parabs and the entire verification work was conducted by Clément Allain.

The Chase-Lev work-stealing deque, a key building block, is based on the implementation in the \Saturn library~\citep*{saturn}, written by Tom Kelly and further improved by Vesa Karvonen. The scheduler is 
largely inspired by the \Domainslib implementation~\citep*{domainslib}, with also some inspiration from \TaskFlow~\citep*{DBLP:journals/tpds/HuangLLL22}. Gabriel Scherer implemented and interpreted the benchmarks, documented some interfaces, and improved the scheduler behavior to eliminate a form of busy-waiting on futures.

The verification work of Clément Allain would not have been possible without the work of the \Iris development team. Our anonymous reviewers asked pointed questions about the specifications and verified examples, which helped improve the specifications.

The writing the article is joint work from both authors, with large parts derived from the PhD manuscript of Clément Allain.
